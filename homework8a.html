Homework 8a: interactive story assignment

Fashion Week Disaster is an interactive story about a young fashion designer preparing for their first major runway show during Fashion Week. The night before the event, multiple problems begin to unfold. A lead model quits unexpectedly, damaged fabrics threaten the collection, and rumors spread online accusing the designer of copying another artist’s work. The player must make difficult choices under pressure while trying to save both the runway show and their reputation.The story takes place in backstage dressing rooms, fashion studios, and the runway itself. Players choose how they respond to challenges throughout the story, including whether to focus on repairing the show or protecting their public image. Different choices lead to different endings, including becoming a viral fashion success, earning recognition as a fashion icon, suffering public failure, or abandoning the fashion industry entirely.
