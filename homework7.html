Homework 7: gifs

For my GIF animation, I made a blinking eye sequence using artwork I had previously created in Adobe Illustrator before bringing it into Photoshop. The original design included eyelashes, but I decided to remove them so the eye itself would stand out more clearly and also it was too difficult of a task. I separated different parts of the illustration onto individual layers, which made the animation process easier to manage. Using Photoshop’s Timeline feature, I built a frame-by-frame animation by adjusting the eyelid positions over multiple frames to create the effect of the eye opening and closing. I kept the animation looping continuously, trying different speeds and then used simple movements to make the motion appear smooth.
